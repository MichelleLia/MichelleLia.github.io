The following people are figures that I am inspired by. Every person has a different story on how each of them had contributed great findings that influenced the world.

Hedy Lamarr



Hedy Lamarr was an actress that was not only talented in the acting industry, but also in inventing new products that transformed the path of technology. She starred in many famous films, such as the Algiers and Sampson and Deliah. She was born in Austria, and emigrated to the United States near the beginning of World War II. One of her most famous inventions was WIFI and GPS. She was an inventor during the 20th century and her contributions remain just as important in the current 21st century.
